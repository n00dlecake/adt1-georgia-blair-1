The Trust

Devoted to the continued support of our friends and family who are Breast cancer survivors!

  • The Start

    Our Beginnings

    The Trust behind Cafe Re: was started by a small local group with a friend struggling to raise funds for reconstructive surgery post mastectomy. She had originally opted not to receive this producer however was later struggling with body image which in turn had a huge impact on her mental state. Our founders though there had to be a way to help financially and go further even to provide a community and support network for other locals going through the same thing! And so, the trust was born.

  • Opening Cafe RE:

    A Gathering Place

    We started up Cafe Re: to help fund group projects, meetings and activities via profit re-distribution from the cafe's income. It is also the perfect community space to host meeting and catch ups.

  • Opening the Online Shop

    So you can buy all the things!

    We decided to branch out and start selling Products like, keep cups, tote bags, and t-shirts with original art and designs from our members and other local artists who wanted to help promote the trust.

  • Where We Are Now...

    Currently we are working closely with our local members to ensure we continue to provide a warm and welcoming support network for our family. If you would like to enquire about grant applications through the trust, please use the contact page and we will be in touch with more info and how we can help you!
